About Us

A Trusted Partner in
Nigerian Energy

Negoa Energy Limited is a reputable Nigerian indigenous oil and gas company duly registered and operating with relevant permits and certifications across the sector.

With our principal offices in Nigeria, we provide a comprehensive range of services spanning engineering design, procurement, construction management, oil and gas trading, security services, and technical manpower supply.

We are committed to developing lasting client relationships by providing exceptional stakeholder value in an environment of trust, respect, and unwavering integrity.

What We Do

Our Core Services

Request a Quote
Engineering, Procurement & Construction

Full-service EPCM capabilities covering process design, project management, procurement, and construction for oil and gas facilities including desalters, dehydrators, and separation systems.

Oil & Gas Trading

Trading and distribution of AGO, PMS, LPFO, and Jet A1 products, with expertise in crude and distillates trading, terminal management, and ATK/DPK supply to international airlines.

Technical Manpower Services

Global and domestic staffing solutions for the oil and gas sector, with a pre-screened candidates database delivering qualified professionals to client operations nationwide.

Security Services

Professional security solutions for oil and gas assets, pipelines, and remote facilities. Full turn-key operations including risk management consultancy and protective security management.

Oilfield Services

Comprehensive upstream support including drilling support, well services, equipment supply and maintenance, inspection, and operational excellence for onshore and offshore environments.

Project Management

End-to-end project management expertise ensuring quality delivery, cost control, and schedule compliance for complex oil and gas capital projects across Nigeria and West Africa.
